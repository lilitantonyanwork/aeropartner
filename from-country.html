Super Midsize

Super midsize private jets are business aircraft known for spacious cabins, impressive range, and advanced avionics. Typically accommodating 8 to 12 passengers, they balance the large cabin space of heavy jets with the efficiency of midsize models. Luxurious amenities include full lavatories, Wi-Fi, and comfortable seating. With ranges up to 4,000 nautical miles, they can fly nonstop between major cities, ideal for transcontinental and international travel.
